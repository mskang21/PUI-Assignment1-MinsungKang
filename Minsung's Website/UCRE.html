Duration: January 2023 ~ March 2023

Collaborator: Leyi Han, Edgar Chen, Minsung Kang, Guanjie Cheng, Elizabeth Wang

In the Spring of 2023, I begun a research project a course called "User Centre Research Evaluation." The aim of the project was to reduce biases in AIs by coming up with a high fidelity design prototype that is based off of rigorous research process that takes the whole semester. My team decided to design this project by thinking about what plugin we could use for Youtube. This plugin aimed to reduce the biases in ad selecting method that Youtube uses to expose users to advertisements before videos

5. How Might We questions

From the areas we would like to improve from Youtube platform, we came up with three main How Might We Statements: How might we make the information on the platform less biased while helping users to engage in contents of their interests?; How might we improve the personalizing ad preferences on Youtube?; How might be enable users to get more familiar with the ad reporting process on Youtube?
